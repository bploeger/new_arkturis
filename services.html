Markets We Serve

Arkturis web and cloud-based solutions have been developed for a wide variety of clients over the years. If your company happens to fall outside of these industries, we welcome your technology request to determine how we can help you too.

Staffing and Employment Services

Developed an intelligent, automated email marketing system that averages 500,000+ emails to a database of over 2 million. The system is designed to match job seekers with open job orders, email the job seeker, then track results. Recruiters are alerted when a job seeker is emailed about their jobs and when a job seeker takes action; i.e. opening the email or click on a link to the job.

Entertainment and Nightclub

Developed a SMS based alert system that allowed club patrons to text message a custom number to opt themselves into receiving text message updates. Club owners could quickly segment and create customized messages to be automatically sent to their database from their smart phone.

Legal and Financial Consulting

Developed an enterprise phone solution to remotely manage and support a mission critical call center. Problems can be diagnosed and fixed from any device that has internet access.

Cloud-based Target Market Research

Developed a system that enabled client to develop custom profiles based upon shopping cart data, and advertise specific products along with test custom messages with the addition of variant testing. Results were tracked and winning combinations were determined based upon revenue generated.

Informational and Ecommerce Websites

Developed informational and ecommerce, shopping cart-based websites for clients in multiple industries including: golf course turf equipment, antiques, medical, legal, rigging, HVAC, leisure, tourism, home improvement and many more!

Customized Cloud-based Applications

Developed a customized cloud-based application for a health care recruitment firm.
